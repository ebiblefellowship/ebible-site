How to Listen by Phone

Dial +1 209-647-1600 and enter the access code 18-16-10 followed by the # key (this is a new service as of 27-November-2011). Note that toll charges or minutes may apply depending on your phone plan as this is NOT a toll-free number.

How to Listen with Skype

Just click to call the freeconferencecallhd.1600 Skype user. Click the dialpad button (small grid) in Skype to open the dialpad and then enter the access code 18-16-10 followed by the # key. Note: If after connecting, the dialpad button is not available, on Windows Skype, click the Call menu item, then click the Show Dial Pad item.
