--- title: Phone Broadcasts description: How to listen to broadcasts by phone ---

Phone Information and Schedule

Throughout the week, live audio is broadcast over a phone confernce which is accessable by phone or Skype. During times of Questions & Answers, the phone conference will be opened up to take questions.

How to Listen by Phone

Dial +1 641-715-3650 (new number as of 19-August-2015) and enter the access code 18-16-10 followed by the # key. During a live Questions and Answers session, to get in line to ask a question, press *6 and then 1. Note that toll charges or minutes may apply depending on your phone plan as this is NOT a toll-free number.

How to Listen by VoIP Dialer

To use the free internet based VoIP Dialer (formerly Flash Phone), navigate to FCC HD Conferencing, click the icon, Dial in via our VoIP Dialer, select the phone number 641-715-3650 (new number as of 19-August-2015) and enter the access code 181610 then enter your name. After clicking the Place Call button, click the Allow button in the small dialog to continue and connect. For more detailed instructions, see VoIP Dialer Help. Note that no signup is required nor any special software to use this service. During a live Questions and Answers session, to get in line to ask a question, press *6 and then 1.

How to Listen with a Smart Phone App

Android and iPhone apps are available. See https://www.freeconferencecallhd.com/hd-conferencing/.

Skype Support

The Skype gateway is no longer supported. If you have a paid Skype plan, follow the instructions above for dial-up access. Otherwise use the VoIP Dialer as previously described.

Schedule

Available at the following times (Eastern/US time):

  • Sunday 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM (except fourth Sunday of each month)
  • Sunday 10:00 AM to 3:30 PM (only the fourth Sunday of each month)
  • Monday through Friday 8:30 PM to 12:30 AM
