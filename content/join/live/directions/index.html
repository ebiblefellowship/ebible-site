DRIVING DIRECTIONS TO P.A.C.E. Union Hall located at 1510 Palmer Drive in Linwood, PA

COMING FROM PHILADELPHIA

Take I-95 South heading towards Delaware. 

Take the Chichester Ave. exit #1 off of 95 (note: This is the last exit in PA before you reach the Delaware state line). 

As you come off of the exit bear left (you will need to make a left turn in order to reach Chichester Ave.)

Turn left onto Chichester ave. (there will be a sign directly in front of you for Marcus Hook with an arrow: follow the direction of the arrow towards Marcus Hook).

Go to the next traffic light on Chichester Ave: this will be Huddell ave.  Turn right onto Huddell ave. 

Go about 50 feet until you reach a stop sign: there will be a driveway on your left called---Palmer Dr.  Turn left onto Palmer Dr. 

Follow Palmer Dr. until you reach an old stone building with an enfenced parking lot: this is the P.A.C.E. Union Hall; Pull into the parking lot and come in. 

 

COMING FROM WILMINGTON

Take I-95 north

Take the Chichester ave. exit #1 off of 95 (note: this will be the first exit after crossing into PA)

As you come off of the exit you will turn right onto Chichester ave.(Towards Marcus Hook). 

FROM THIS POINT ON FOLLOW DIRECTIONS GIVEN ABOVE!
