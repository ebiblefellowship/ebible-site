EBible Fellowship Broadcasting Information

EBible Fellowship maintains several PalTalk chat rooms into which we broadcast various live and prerecorded programming. For some of these PalTalk rooms, there is also corresponding MP3 and Ogg Vorbis streams as an alternative way to listen.

Live Teaching

Description: Various live messages and Bible Q&A’s are broadcast on this stream using a direct feed from the live audio source. As a result, this stream will typically be better quality than the Bible Teaching streams below which are relayed through Paltalk.

MP3 Stream: Low to medium bandwidth 32 to 64 Kbps stream depending on the broadcast
http://audiocast.ebiblefellowship.com:8008/live.mp3.m3u

Bible Teaching and Music

Description: Various times of live messages and Bible Q&A. Prerecorded messages from our web site and music are played into the room at other times.

PalTalk Category: Religion and Theology, Christianity

PalTalk Room Name: EBible Fellowship

Hours of programming: Open 24 hours, 7 days a week (as PalTalk allows). Please see our live schedule. Prerecorded messages and music are broadcast at other times.

Room Information and Status: View

MP3 Stream: Low bandwidth 24 Kbps stream suitable for dial-up users.
http://audiocast.ebiblefellowship.com:8008/teaching-low.mp3.m3u

Ogg Vorbis Stream: Medium bandwidth 50 Kbps stream suitable for broadband users.
http://audiocast.ebiblefellowship.com:8008/teaching.ogg.m3u
