So in Luke 21:26, it says:

Men’s hearts failing them for fear, and for looking after those things which are coming on the earth…

It is coming, and yet there is already “signs in the sun, and in the moon, and in the stars.” There is already “distress of nations, with perplexity.” “The sea and the waves” are already “roaring.” But then it says “which are coming.”

How is this possible? It is possible because we will be able to see this happening in New Zealand. We will be able to see this happening in Japan, in the Philippines, in Australia. As it gets closer and closer and closer, everyone will be looking at what is coming directly for them. It will be coming right for us and we will be able to see this.

Luke 21:26-28 says that we will be:

…looking after those things which are coming on the earth: for the powers of heaven shall be shaken. And then shall they see the Son of man coming in a cloud…

They will not see Him literally, but they will know that this is God’s doing. It continues:

…with power and great glory. And when these things begin to come to pass, then look up, and lift up your heads; for your redemption draweth nigh.

This is another verse that we never really understood. We are understanding verses now that we never really understood before. We wondered how this could be happening “when these things begin to come to pass” and when God is telling the believers to “look up.”

We now understand that this is happening while we are still here. Plus, men’s hearts are “failing them for fear,” and yet God is encouraging the true believers not to worry: “Look up… for your redemption draweth nigh.” The only answer to these verses is the rolling earthquake idea, that we will see this happening. It will start across the other side of the world and then it will approach us.

There is one more verse I would like us to go to. We read in Psalm 46:1-3:

God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble. Therefore will not we fear, though the earth be removed, and though the mountains be carried into the midst of the sea; Though the waters thereof roar and be troubled, though the mountains shake with the swelling thereof. Selah.

This sounds very much like an earthquake and a tsunami. When the water comes into the land for several miles and there are hills and mountains that the water is swirling around, the mountains are being carried into the water, which would fit this description.

Again, this says “the sea and the waves roaring,” because we have seen demonstrations of what happens with the ocean water when there is an earthquake; yet God is saying of His people, “Therefore will not we fear.”

We are in the East Coast and we will be able to see this coming and coming. For the true believer, the Bible says, “Then look up, and lift up your heads; for your redemption draweth nigh.”

This is good news, because God is indicating that His people will not fear. His people will not be afraid of this. The instructions to the “locusts” are “that they should not hurt the grass of the earth, neither any green thing.”

We had also wondered about this verse and what it meant when it said that no “green thing” was to be hurt. We now understand that this is referring to the true believers and that God is giving instruction that this is not going to harm them. This is not going to hurt the true believers at all. As we see this coming, we are not to be afraid.

I think that God’s people will not be afraid, because God will make sure of this. We will know then that this is it, and yet we know this now.

This is kind of like John the Baptist. He knew that Christ was the Saviour. He knew that Christ was the Messiah, but he still sent a few of his disciples to ask Jesus, “Art thou he that should come, or do we look for another?” It is not that he did not know this, but he was in prison, things were a little rough for him, and so he just wanted some words of reassurance. He wanted some additional words of confirmation, even though he knew this full well.

Likewise, this is how it will be with the people of God when we actually see this happening. We knew that this was going to happen, but then God will just absolutely confirm this, because it will be happening before our eyes.

Let us stop here.
