Questions and Answers from the Bible

Each Sunday afternoon at about 1:30 PM ET and Monday and Friday evenings at 9:30 PM ET, EBible Fellowship hosts a Questions and Answers session. During this time, anyone can ask questions relating to the Bible.

Here's how you can join us:

  • If you’re calling on the phone, dial +1 209-647-1600 and enter the access code 18-16-10 followed by the # key to get into the conference. To get in line to ask a question, press *6 and then 1.
  • To use the free internet based Flash Phone, navigate to FCC HD Conferencing, click the icon, Dial in via our Flash Phone, select the phone number 209-647-1600, enter the access code 181610 and enter your name. After clicking the Place Call button, click the Allow button in the small dialog to continue and connect. For more detailed instructions, see Flash Phone Help. Note that no signup is required for this service. To get in line to ask a question, press *6 and then 1.
  • If you’re on Skype, just click to call the freeconferencecallhd.1600 Skype user. Click the dialpad button (it looks like a small grid) in Skype to open the dialpad and then enter the access code 18-16-10 followed by the # key. (Note: If after connecting, the dialpad button is not available, on Windows Skype, click the Call menu item, then click the Show Dial Pad item.) To get in line to ask a question, press *6 and then 1.
  • If you’re on Paltalk, please enter the EBible Fellowship room (directions on how to do that here). Once there, please raise your hand, and when asked to, please post your question, and your question will be relayed by one of the admins there.

(NOTE: during the fourth Sunday Q&A’s, you can only ask questions by Paltalk).

MP3 recordings of past Q&A times along with their transcripts are archived here for you to listen to and read.
