Questions and Answers from the Bible

Each Sunday afternoon at about 1:30 PM ET and Monday and Friday evenings at 9:30 PM ET, EBible Fellowship hosts a Questions and Answers session. During this time, anyone can ask questions relating to the Bible.

Here’s how you can join us:

  • If you’re calling on the phone, dial +1 641-715-3650 and enter the access code 18-16-10 followed by the # key to get into the conference (the phone number has changed as of 19-August-2015). To get in line to ask a question, press *6 and then 1.
  • To use the free internet based VoIP Dialer (formerly Flash Phone), navigate to FCC HD Conferencing, click the icon, Dial in via our VoIP Dialer, select the phone number 641-715-3650, enter the access code 181610 and enter your name (the phone number has changed as of 19-August-2015). After clicking the Place Call button, click the Allow button in the small dialog to continue and connect. For more detailed instructions, see VoIP Dialer Help. Note that no signup nor any special software is required to use this service. To get in line to ask a question, press *6 and then 1.
  • Android and iPhone apps are available. See FCC HD Conferencing.
  • The Skype gateway is no longer supported. If you have a paid Skype plan, follow the instructions above for calling on a phone. Otherwise use the VoIP Dialer as previously described.
  • If you’re on Paltalk, please enter the EBible Fellowship room (directions on how to do that here). Once there, please raise your hand, and when asked to, please post your question, and your question will be relayed by one of the admins there.

(NOTE: during the fourth Sunday Q&A’s, you can only ask questions by Paltalk).

MP3 recordings of past Q&A times along with their transcripts are archived here for you to listen to and read.
