The Number 84

by Robin Harp

The number 84 is seen in the duration of the great tribuation. Consider the following:

  • Jacob’s tribulation - 1st historical type of the great tribulation was from 1879 B.C. to 1872 B.C. = 7 years X 12 months = 84 months
  • Judah’s tribulation - 2nd historical type of the great tribulation was from 609 B.C. to 539 B.C. = 70 years X 12 months = 840 months
  • Today’s tribulation - 3rd and actual great tribulation is from 1988 A.D. to 2011 A.D. = 23 years X 365.2422 days = 8400 days
