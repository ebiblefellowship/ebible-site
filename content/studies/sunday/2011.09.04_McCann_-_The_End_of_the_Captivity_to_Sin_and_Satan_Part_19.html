Let us look at one other thing. I think that this may explain this in living color. In Ezekiel 37, we read of the valley of dry bones. I have read this a lot of times. With some of the language, it has been hard to place the groups of people because of the way this is worded. This is such a great chapter, so let us begin in verse one. We read in Ezekiel 37:1-7:

The hand of JEHOVAH was upon me, and carried me out in the spirit of JEHOVAH, and set me down in the midst of the valley which was full of bones, And caused me to pass by them round about: and, behold, there were very many in the open valley; and, lo, they were very dry. And he said unto me, Son of man, can these bones live? And I answered, O Lord JEHOVAH, thou knowest. Again he said unto me, Prophesy upon these bones, and say unto them, O ye dry bones, hear the word of JEHOVAH. Thus saith the Lord JEHOVAH unto these bones; Behold, I will cause breath to enter into you, and ye shall live: And I will lay sinews upon you, and will bring up flesh upon you, and cover you with skin, and put breath in you, and ye shall live; and ye shall know that I am JEHOVAH. So I prophesied as I was commanded: and as I prophesied, there was a noise, and behold a shaking…

This is translated in some places as “earthquake.” And so there is an earthquake. And, of course, on May 21 there was spiritually a great earthquake.

Ezekiel 37:7 continues on to say:

…and the bones came together, bone to his bone.

Bone to whose bone? This says, “bone to his bone.” It is to His bone, because the Bible says that we are bone of His bone and flesh of His flesh. And so this is the body of Christ that is forming. As the parts come together, this is the completion of all of God’s elect.

Then we read in Ezekiel 37:8-10:

And when I beheld, lo, the sinews and the flesh came up upon them, and the skin covered them above: but there was no breath in them. Then said he unto me, Prophesy unto the wind, prophesy, son of man, and say to the wind, Thus saith the Lord JEHOVAH; Come from the four winds, O breath, and breathe upon these slain, that they may live. So I prophesied as he commanded me, and the breath came into them, and they lived, and stood up upon their feet, an exceeding great army.

It says in the literal translation that this is “a very great army” or an army of very, very many. It has a strong emphasis that there are quite a number of them.

Another way of saying this is that this was a great multitude, which we read about in Revelation 7. This is referring to the 200 million, except this is not all 200 million in view here. Just like 200 million were not saved out of the great tribulation. A vast majority of them were, but yet there were still some others.

Then it goes on to say in Ezekiel 37:11:

Then he said unto me, Son of man, these bones are the whole house of Israel: behold, they say, Our bones are dried, and our hope is lost: we are cut off for our parts.

This says that this is “the whole house.” This means that what is in view here are all of the elect with none left. These are the bones that are being referred to here, but we assumed that these were just the bones that we were reading about that were in the open valley. But this is strange language where it says in Ezekiel 37:11:

…our hope is lost: we are cut off for our parts.

I think that this reads better as:

…we are cut off to ourselves [or from ourselves].

God’s elect, the great multitude, just came out of great tribulation, many of whom are still alive. Are we cut off from ourselves? In one way, yes, because there is still this battle between the flesh and the spirit, but this is not what this means.

Who is cut off from themselves? This is referring to the souls in Heaven. The physical part of them, which is their bodies, is still on this earth. They are still separated from themselves. But there is a change here. There are these bones that are in the open valley, but then look at Ezekiel 37:12. This says:

Therefore prophesy and say unto them, Thus saith the Lord JEHOVAH; Behold, O my people, I will open your graves, and cause you to come up out of your graves, and bring you into the land of Israel.

We can read “Heaven” here.

Then we read in Ezekiel 37:13-14:

And ye shall know that I am JEHOVAH, when I have opened your graves, O my people, and brought you up out of your graves, And shall put my spirit in you, and ye shall live, and I shall place you in your own land: then shall ye know that I JEHOVAH have spoken it, and performed it, saith JEHOVAH.

What is the difference here? There is a difference. What is the difference between these bones and the bones that stood on their feet? God begins talking to bones that are in the grave. They are in the grave. They are not lying in the open valley. The “exceeding great army” was lying in an open valley, but these are in the grave.

This is what happened. God saved a great multitude and then it is like He turns to the dead who are still in the ground. He is saying that He just finished His salvation plan. He has saved them all and these are “the whole house of Israel.” But we can know assuredly that He is going to raise His elect up. This is guaranteed. This is another phase. Since He has done this, they can know that He is not going to forget them. He will remember them and raise them from the dead and give them their new resurrected bodies.

In other words, to put all of this together with Revelation 9 and the 200 million who are bound in the great river Euphrates, which is a synonym for Babylon and is referring to this world, God has saved all of the elect, including those whose bodies are still in the ground and whose souls are in Heaven. He saved them all. This act of saving them all is what brings torment on the unsaved of the world who had nothing to do with the church, and this is what slays the “third part,” because there are no more names written in the Lamb’s Book of Life to become saved; and yet this did not take a literal resurrection or a literal rapture to do this and to accomplish this. At the same time, He is assuring all of those who are in Heaven that He will complete this at some point, which will be on October 21, 2011.

I know that this is a lot. There are a lot of verses to consider. But we have to understand that God is speeding up this process. We do not have too much time left to really lay all of these things out in a nice methodical way. We do not have time to do this.
