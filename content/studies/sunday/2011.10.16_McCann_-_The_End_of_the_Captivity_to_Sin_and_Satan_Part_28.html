This is what God is about to do. This is what we are looking forward to in a few days, the completion of God’s salvation plan. Finally, God’s people will enter into Heaven in totality, all of the elect dwelling with Him forevermore and partaking of the tree of life to receive eternal life, that gift that comes completely by His grace and completely as a result of what Christ has done for His people.

In closing, let us go back to Malachi 4. I will read Malachi 4:1 again:

For, behold, the day cometh, that shall burn as an oven; and all the proud, yea, and all that do wickedly, shall be stubble: and the day that cometh shall burn them up, saith JEHOVAH of hosts, that it shall leave them neither root nor branch.

They will not have Christ who is typified by a root. He is also typified by a branch in the Old Testament. They are not going to have Christ.

Isaiah 5:24 tells us about their root. Their root is not going to help them. This says:

Therefore as the fire devoureth the stubble, and the flame consumeth the chaff, so their root shall be as rottenness, and their blossom shall go up as dust: because they have cast away the law of JEHOVAH of hosts, and despised the word of the Holy One of Israel.

So this root is of no help at all, nor the branch. Remember that Jesus is typified as the branch, but so are followers of Christ.

We read in John 15:6:

If a man abide not in me, he is cast forth as a branch, and is withered; and men gather them, and cast them into the fire, and they are burned.

Again, we have to be in Christ. We need Him within us. We need true salvation and God has to work this out. Man cannot get himself saved and never could. And now there is no possibility of salvation at all.

Going back to Malachi 4:2, it says:

But unto you that fear my name shall the Sun of righteousness arise with healing in his wings; and ye shall go forth, and grow up as calves of the stall.

We saw before how there is spiritual darkness in all of the earth, but in one place there is light. This one place is within the great multitude whom God has saved, as it says in Isaiah 60:1-2:

Arise, shine; for thy light is come, and the glory of JEHOVAH is risen upon thee. For, behold, the darkness shall cover the earth, and gross darkness the people: but JEHOVAH shall arise upon thee, and his glory shall be seen upon thee.

This is where the light is. It is arising within the hearts of those who were previously in darkness in their souls, but God saved them.

So the Sun of righteousness is arising with healing in His wings. We saw that the tree is for the healing of the nations. The waters go forth. Wherever they go, they bring life and healing. This is the nature of the Gospel. This is what God has done.

One last thing is where it says in Malachi 4:2:

…ye shall go forth, and grow up as calves of the stall.

Look at Amos 6:3-4:

Ye that put far away the evil day, and cause the seat of violence to come near; That lie upon beds of ivory, and stretch themselves upon their couches, and eat the lambs out of the flock, and the calves out of the midst of the stall;

The only reason I read this is to show that lambs of the flock and calves of the stall are synonyms.

We know that, again and again, God is referring to feeding sheep at this time. He is the Good Shepherd who leads His people, as we saw with Moses leading Israel through the Red Sea. He is leading us out of the sheepfold. After He gathered the last one, the door of the sheepfold shut and He is leading us to the Promised Land.

We can understand this as we just have this image better in our minds:

…ye shall go forth, and grow up as [lambs of the flock].

This is what God is doing now at this time.

We will stop here.
