EBible Fellowship is sending Bibles to needy recipients. If you cannot afford to purchase your own Bible or cannot obtain one and are sincerely interested in learning more about the Word of God, please fill out this form and we will consider your request. As purchasing and mailing costs are rather high, we reserve the right to only send Bibles to those who most need them and only send one Bible to each household address.

Currently, we are receiving far more requests than we can fulfill. Because of this, we place a great weight on what you type in for the reason you would like a Bible. Please enter a meaningful comment for us to consider your request.

You can monitor the availability of free Bibles in English and Russian by subscribing to one of these RSS feeds. You can learn more about RSS feeds here.

 RSS feed to monitor English Bible availability
 RSS feed to monitor Russian Bible availability

Due to overwhelming demand and limited funds, we can no longer accept new requests for Portuguese Bibles at this time.

We plan to start accepting requests again starting next month so please check back at that time.

With the many requests we are receiving for Bibles, we welcome anyone who would like to support our ministry with a donation.

We ask you only make one request for free materials per month. If you have made a request for some other item on our site within the past month, please wait 30 days before requesting another item.
