EBible Fellowship’s Fellowship Hour Archives

“Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some is; but exhorting one another: and so much the more, as ye see the day approaching.” Hebrews 10:25

Each Sunday evening from 9:00 to 10:00 PM ET, EBible Fellowship hosts its Fellowship Hour. During this time we gather together in the EBible Fellowship PalTalk room where Chris McCann endeavors to answer all questions relating to the Bible.

MP3 recordings of the Fellowship Hour along with their transcripts are archived here for you to listen to and read. Since late July of 2006, dedicated volunteers have been transcribing each Fellowship Hour. These transcripts are normally available by the following Tuesday morning.

Recordings are normally posted on the web site within an hour after the Fellowship Hour has ended. Fully edited recordings are normally posted as early as two hours later or by the following Tuesday morning.
